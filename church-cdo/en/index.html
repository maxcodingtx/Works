About Us

God awaits you with open arms!

The mission of Iglesia Casa de Oración is to celebrate through worship, Proclaim salvation through Jesus Christ, promote spiritual growth, spread the word of Jesus Christ through good deeds and to minister by God's love.

We are a family!

We believe in following the example of Jesus Christ. This includes feeding the hungry, helping the needy, clothing the naked, celebrating mass, serving the church and serving in our communities all around us. Come join us!

You are Invited!

We are a community committed to loving God, loving people, and striving to answer the call to know Him and to make Him known. We're excited to have you join us. Come As You Are! The hurting, the lost, the broken - everyone is welcome here. We want you to join us just as you are and experience the hope we have in Christ.
